Frequently asked questions

Is this 100% free?
It is free and it will be free forever. But if you want you can donate us money via Kofi below.
Where can is see the uptime of this server?
The status is currently found on Status Page 1, and on Status Page 2 (http only)
Are my passwords secure?
So secure as a self-hosted vaultwarden instance would be. This is just a normal Vaultwarden server in a Kubernetes Cluster. We cannot see your passwords and cannot access your vault.
Can you delete my account or my passwords?
Yes we can, but we don't do it. However, we encourage you to do not trust us and backup your vault frequently. We reserve the right to delete accounts that have no passwords and have no activity.
What data do you save? Is there any tracking?
We save everything what vaultwarden saves in its default configuration. Data we have from you is only the data your provide us and your IP Address and the URL you requested. There is no tracking of your activities beyond the default. We keep logs for 4 days.
Other instances
There are also other instances, so go check them out! You can checkout this tool to migrate your data: Portwarden (No warranty)
